Our Journey

Born From a Simple Obsession for Mangoes

MangoMania began with a simple passion: creating the perfect mango lassi. What started as a family recipe and a love for authentic mango flavors has grown into one of East London's favorite destinations for fresh mango drinks, desserts, and treats.

Located on Mile End Road, we have spent years perfecting every recipe, focusing on quality, consistency, and unforgettable flavor. Our mission has always been simple — to celebrate the world's finest mangoes and share their natural sweetness with our community.

At the heart of everything we create is the legendary Alphonso mango, widely known as the "King of Mangoes." Sourced during peak season and blended with thick, creamy Greek yogurt and premium ingredients, our drinks are made without artificial flavors, concentrates, or preservatives.

Today, our outstanding 4.9-star Google rating reflects the trust and support of the diverse community we proudly serve. From local residents and university students to tourists and families, every customer is welcomed with the same warmth, care, and passion that inspired our very first recipe.
